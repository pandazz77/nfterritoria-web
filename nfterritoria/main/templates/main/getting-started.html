Frequently Asked Questions

How to start playing?

In order for you to start playing, you will need a white list of NFTs, territories and miners that can only be placed on your territory. You can get all this on the "Sales" page, and after the sale on the market. Go to the "My NFT" page, log in and stake your NFTs, then get a password to log in to the server.
Download our launcher and with your password you will get into the wonderful world of NFTerritoria!
To get starting equipment, enter the command "/nft kit start".

How to get territory?

Take possession of the NFT "territory", then transfer them to the game on the" My NFT " page. when you enter the game and write a command to capture the territory "/nft claim (16 or 8)", by placing it you will capture the territory around you with the radius specified in the characteristics of the NFT. Congratulations, now you officially own a piece of land in the world of NFTerritoria!

How to start mining?

Take possession of the NFT miners, then transfer them to the game on the "My NFT" page. When you enter the game you will need to type the command "/nft get" to pick up the miners from the temporary storage. Miners must be located on your territory. Each block has unique characteristics, which you can learn about on the "Miners" page. After you have found out what your miner needs to extract tokens, go to the farm resources zone to search for the items you need. Have you extracted resources? Great, now you can start mining. Put fuel and a certain resource in the slots, you will see how your miner started mining tokens!

How to unstake my NFT, tokens?

Take the item you need in your hand, then write the command "/nft unstake"to the chat. The item will disappear from your hand and will be sent to your wallet soon. In the case of tokens, you need to write the same command, the tokens will disappear from your inventory and will be placed in our temporary storage. You can collect tokens from the storage on the "My NFT" page by switching the page content to "staked". In the upper-right corner of the page, you will see the number of your frozen tokens, under this information there is a button "unstake all tokens", when you click on this button, all tokens will be transferred to your wax wallet.

FAQ

Q: What NFT items do I need to start playing?
A: In order to play on our server, you will need to purchase a whitelist token, and to start mining our token, you will need to purchase a complete set - whitelist token, territory, miner.

Q: What should I do in the game?
A: You have to place your territory and place your miner on it, and then go in search of fuel for your miner on the opposite shore.

Q: What are the main commands you need to know to start the game?
"/nft get" - get staked nft from temporary storage.
A: "/nft claim (16 or 8)" - place the territory around you.
"/nft unstake" - send the item in your hand to your wallet.

Q: I want to respawn, how can I do it?
A: Use command "/nft respawn". Be careful - your character will die.

Q: How can I move between the shores?
A: You can get around by boat or by ferrymen, which you can find along the coast.
