Frequently Asked Questions

How to start playing?

In order for you to start playing, you will need a white list of NFTs, territories and miners that can only be placed on your territory. You can get all this on the "Sales" page, and after the sale on the market. Go to the "My NFT" page, log in and send the received NFTs to the bid, then get a password to log in to the server.
Download our launcher and with the help of a code phrase you will get into the wonderful world of NFTerritoria!

How to get territory?

Take possession of the NFT "territory", then transfer them to the game on the" My NFT " page. when you enter the game and write a command to capture the territory "/nft claim (16 or 8)", by placing it you will capture the territory around you with the radius specified in the characteristics of the NFT. Congratulations, now you officially own a piece of land in the world of NFTerritoria!

How to start mining?

Take the item you need in your hand, then write the command "/nft unstake"to the chat. The item will disappear from your hand and will be sent to your wallet soon. In the case of tokens, you need to write the same command, the tokens will disappear from your inventory and will be placed in our temporary storage. You can collect tokens from the storage on the "My NFT" page by switching the page content to "staked". In the upper right corner of the page, you will see the number of your tokens that have been transferred to the server, under this information there is a button "unstake all tokens", clicking on which all tokens will be transferred to your wax wallet.

How to unstake my NFT, tokens?

Take the item you need in your hand, then write the command "/nft unstake"to the chat. The item will disappear from your hand and will be sent to your wallet soon. In the case of tokens, you need to write the same command, the tokens will disappear from your inventory and will be placed in our temporary storage. You can collect tokens from the storage on the "My NFT" page by switching the page content to "staked". In the upper-right corner of the page, you will see the number of your frozen tokens, under this information there is a button "unstake all tokens", when you click on this button, all tokens will be transferred to your wax wallet.
